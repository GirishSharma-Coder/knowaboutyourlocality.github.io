About Us

Namaste, and welcome to our expat communities in Pune, the home of vada pav, as well as some of the most captivating culture and ancient history on the planet. A combination of this impressive past and an exciting new generation of entrepreneurs and small businesses is what makes Pune so special, and is driving its position in the world as a global economic power. Historically, expats have populated the beautiful city of Pune for the past few decades, and continue to do so today. The culture may vary a lot from what expats are used to in their home country, but the people have always been exceptionally friendly. If you're wondering, "How will I adjust to the culture of Pune", "How different is the culture of Pune" or "Is Pune's culture difficult to adjust to?", then you have come to the right place, as we will provide all the details you need to know before coming to the wondeful city of Pune.
